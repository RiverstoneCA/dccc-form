儿童汉语阅读障碍量表
Dyslexia Checklist for Chinese Children (DCCC)
吴汉荣等编制 · 华中科技大学同济心理卫生研究中心 · 适用于小学三至五年级
基本信息 · Basic Information
填写说明：本表对所列的57个条目分别规定了5个等级，请根据孩子实际情况选择最接近的答案。每题只选一个答案。
Instructions: Rate each of the 57 items on a 5-point scale. Select one answer per item.
1 从未出现 Never 2 偶尔出现 Rarely 3 有时出现 Sometimes 4 较常出现 Often 5 经常出现 Always
观察项目 · Observation Items (57 items)
1
经常混淆字母：如将b看成d，p看成q，u看成n，w看成m等。
Often confuses letters (e.g., b/d, p/q, u/n, w/m).
2
经常颠倒字的偏旁部首。
Often reverses radicals in characters.
3
阅读时重复阅读同一行或者跳行阅读。
Repeats reading the same line or skips lines when reading.
4
上课或做作业时注意力不集中。
Has difficulty concentrating in class or while doing homework.
5
放大字体、减少每页内容或用物件标记读到哪里可以改善阅读。
Enlarging font, reducing content per page, or marking reading position can improve reading.
6
读字和写字时经常混淆形状相似的字，如拒和柜。
Frequently confuses similarly shaped characters, such as 拒 and 柜.
7
前后排列错误，例如将was看成saw，将on看成no，将书写看成写书。
Mistakes in order, such as seeing was as saw, on as no, or shu xie as xie shu.
8
听写中分不清同音字，如拒和据。
Confuses homophones in dictation, such as 拒 and 据.
9
写字字迹非常潦草，笔划不清晰，难以辨认。
Handwriting is often very poor and difficult to read.
10
常常不理解字、词在句子中的意思。
Often does not understand the meaning of characters/words in sentences.
11
分不清汉字的声调，如情（第二声），清（第一声）。
Cannot distinguish tonal differences, like 情 (2nd tone) and 清 (1st tone).
12
计数困难，数学计算能力差。
Has difficulty counting and poor math calculation ability.
13
父母或其他家庭成员也有阅读、语言或书写方面的问题。
Parents or family members also have reading, language, or writing issues.
14
看图时，抓不住主要内容，只看到琐碎细节。
When looking at pictures, fails to grasp the main content, only noticing trivial details.
15
口头交际能力差，不善于口语表达。
Poor oral communication skills, not good at verbal expression.
16
书写速度慢，经常很晚才完成作业。
Slow writing speed, often finishes homework very late.
17
听不懂正常速度的谈话，只有缓慢重复时才能理解。
Cannot understand normal-speed conversations; only comprehends when repeated slowly.
18
不能按照大人的指令做事情。
Cannot follow instructions given by adults.
19
无法用学过的字、词造句子。
Unable to create sentences using learned words and phrases.
20
写字时经常涂抹、修改。
Frequently erases or modifies while writing.
21
阅读过程中常常分不清读音相近的字，如轻和清。
Often cannot distinguish phonetically similar characters, such as 轻 and 清.
22
不能熟练使用汉语拼音。
Cannot proficiently use Chinese Pinyin.
23
听不懂口头讲解，跟不上正常的学习速度。
Does not understand verbal explanations; cannot keep pace with normal learning speed.
24
朗读时经常读着读着不知读到何处。
Often loses track of where they are while reading aloud.
25
不理解上下、周围、首尾、前后、向上和向下等方位概念。
Does not understand spatial concepts such as up/down, surroundings, front/back, etc.
26
认字能力虽好，却不知道字的意义。
Good character recognition but does not know the meaning of characters.
27
儿童难以记住公式、乘法口诀等。
Finds it difficult to remember formulas, multiplication tables, etc.
28
写字容易写错，如总是多一笔或少一笔。
Easily writes errors, such as always adding or omitting strokes.
29
不理解时间关系：如昨天、今天和明天，前与后，15分钟与2小时，快与慢等。
Does not understand time relations: yesterday/today/tomorrow, 15 min vs. 2 hours, fast/slow, etc.
30
没有幽默感，听不懂玩笑话或双关语。
Lacks a sense of humor; does not understand jokes or puns.
31
写作吃力，语文测验时作文分数低。
Writing is laborious; scores low in writing tests.
32
不理解人的情绪，如不领会愉快、反感之类的情绪表现。
Does not understand human emotions, such as happy or disgusted.
33
难以掌握数学概念（例如多与少，大于与小于）；不会估算。
Struggles with math concepts (e.g., more/less, greater than/less than); cannot estimate.
34
重复别人所说的数字时，超不过六位数字。
Cannot repeat numbers stated by others exceeding six digits.
35
熟练掌握的词汇很少。
Has a very limited vocabulary.
36
常常不愿朗读或朗读时发音不清晰。
Often reluctant to read aloud, or mispronounces when reading aloud.
37
朗读时总是丢字、加字、改字、串字。
Consistently omits, adds, changes, or jumbles characters when reading aloud.
38
记不住物品名称，只能说那个东西。
Cannot remember names of objects; only refers to them as that thing.
39
写字常常超出格子。
Handwriting often exceeds the lines/boxes.
40
富于说服力和表现力的语言太少。
Lacks persuasive and expressive language.
41
写作能力差：标点符号、空一行、空两格等等常搞错。
Poor writing skills: often confuses punctuation, spacing, etc.
42
阅读时喜欢出声。
Enjoys reading aloud (prefers to subvocalize when reading).
43
朗读时总是反复重复某些字词。
Often repeats certain words when reading aloud.
44
常常认不出或不知道学过的字是什么意思。
Often fails to recognize or know the meanings of previously learned characters.
45
易记住人名而不易记住人脸。
Easily remembers names but struggles to recognize faces.
46
语文考试时阅读理解部分得分低。
Scores low in reading comprehension during language exams.
47
不喜欢阅读，也不喜欢听人阅读。
Dislikes reading and does not enjoy listening to others read.
48
写字、画画时笔划不均匀，歪歪斜斜。
Writes or draws with uneven strokes, often crooked.
49
孩子不经常阅读课外读物。
Does not frequently read extracurricular books.
50
能正确阅读，但是有口无心，理解较差。
Reads correctly but lacks understanding; poor comprehension.
51
考试或写作业时，常常出现题意理解错误。
Frequently misunderstands questions during exams or homework.
52
阅读写作又慢又差。
Reading and writing are both slow and poor quality.
53
语言表达尚可，但写的作文过于简单，内容枯燥。
Verbal expression is acceptable, but written compositions are too simple and lack content.
54
经常忘记一个学过的字应该怎样写。
Often forgets how to write a previously learned character.
55
读书时常常有看不清楚，或者看到的字有颤抖和闪烁的感觉。
Often sees unclear characters, or experiences shaking/flickering letters when reading.
56
对大人的吩咐，前讲后忘记。
Forgets instructions given by adults shortly after being told.
57
数学应用题常常不能正确解答，数学考试时应用题部分得分低。
Frequently fails to correctly solve math word problems; scores low on applied questions in math exams.
